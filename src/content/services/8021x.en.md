# 802.1X Authentication Deployment and Maintenance

We deliver wired and wireless access control projects that combine RADIUS servers, certificate authorities, and network device integrations. The result is a consistent authentication experience that keeps unauthorized devices off your corporate network.

## What We Deliver
- Readiness assessment, VLAN segmentation planning, and supplicant configuration guidance.
- Deployment of FreeRADIUS, Cisco ISE, or other NAC platforms plus PKI enrollment and lifecycle automation.
- Ongoing maintenance with policy updates, certificate renewals, and compliance reporting.

## Outcomes
- Strong identity-based network access for employees, contractors, and BYOD scenarios.
- Simplified onboarding through self-service certificate provisioning and device profiling.
- Auditable logs demonstrating who connected, when, and under which policy.

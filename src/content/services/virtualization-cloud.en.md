# Virtualization and Cloud Solutions

We modernize legacy infrastructure by consolidating servers onto resilient virtualization platforms or extending your estate with cloud resources. Our engineers specialize in Proxmox VE, VMware alternatives, and GPU-enabled compute for AI or VDI workloads.

## What We Deliver
- Assessment, sizing, and migration plans for moving physical or legacy VMs onto modern clusters.
- Design of HA clusters, storage, and networking with Proxmox VE, Ceph, and SDN integrations.
- Hybrid connectivity, backup, and disaster recovery blueprints that tie on-prem and cloud environments together.

## Outcomes
- Better resource utilization and simplified management through centralized virtualization.
- Reduced licensing costs by adopting open platforms without sacrificing enterprise features.
- A future-ready foundation for AI/ML, VDI, or cloud bursting scenarios.

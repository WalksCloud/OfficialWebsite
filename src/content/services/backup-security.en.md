# Enterprise Backup and Security Solutions

Critical data deserves redundancy and protection from ransomware or accidental loss. We design backup, email security, and monitoring solutions that combine Proxmox components with complementary tooling to ensure business continuity.

## What We Deliver
- Architecture and rollout of Proxmox Backup Server, Mail Gateway, and immutable storage targets.
- Backup policies, retention plans, and recovery playbooks validated through regular testing.
- Integration with monitoring, alerting, and security operations processes for full visibility.

## Outcomes
- Faster recovery times with confidence that backups are complete and uncompromised.
- Reduced attack surface for email-borne threats and data exfiltration attempts.
- Compliance-ready audit trails covering backup jobs, restores, and access history.

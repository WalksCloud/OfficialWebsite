# Identity and Access Management with ZITADEL

ZITADEL delivers modern identity services with an open foundation. We help you adopt it to consolidate logins, enforce zero-trust policies, and give developers flexible APIs for embedding authentication into apps.

## What We Deliver
- IAM strategy, tenant hierarchy, and role modeling aligned with organizational structures.
- Deployment of ZITADEL (cloud or self-hosted), plus federation with legacy IdPs and directories.
- Integration with applications via OIDC/OAuth, SCIM provisioning, and automated governance workflows.

## Outcomes
- Unified SSO and MFA experience for employees, partners, and customers.
- Centralized auditing of authentication events, admin activity, and lifecycle operations.
- Faster rollout of new services thanks to reusable identity components and APIs.

# IT Monitoring and Management Systems

We architect and operate monitoring platforms that let you see every metric, log, and alert across servers, networks, and applications. By combining open-source observability stacks with clear runbooks, we deliver real-time insight into the health of your IT environments.

## What We Deliver
- End-to-end deployment of Zabbix, LibreNMS, Grafana, Graylog, and Wazuh with dashboards tailored to your KPIs.
- Alert rules, escalation policies, and integrations with chat or ticketing tools so incidents reach the right team immediately.
- Platform hardening, backup policies, and upgrade plans that keep the monitoring stack secure and reliable.

## Outcomes
- Faster mean time to detect and resolve infrastructure or application issues.
- Centralized observability data for auditing, capacity planning, and compliance.
- Predictable operations thanks to documentation, runbooks, and training for your internal staff.

# IDC Data Center Deployment and Maintenance

We support your presence inside carrier-neutral data centers with turnkey services that cover rack design, structured cabling, network cores, and 24/7 remote-hands response. Our team handles vendor coordination and compliance so you stay focused on applications.

## What We Deliver
- Capacity and resiliency planning for racks, power feeds, cooling, and structured cabling.
- Deployment of core/aggregation switches, firewalls, and load balancers with high availability.
- Managed services including remote-hands dispatch, inventory tracking, and change documentation.

## Outcomes
- Faster time-to-service when scaling infrastructure footprints in new or existing IDCs.
- Predictable operations backed by documented procedures and SLA-based support.
- Auditable environments that align with security and regulatory requirements.
